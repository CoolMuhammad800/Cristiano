About:

Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro is a Portuguese professional footballer who plays as a forward for and captains both Saudi Pro League club Al Nassr and the Portugal national team.

Ronaldo's Past:

Ronaldo grew up in an impoverished Roman Catholic home, sharing a room with all his siblings. As a child, Ronaldo played for Andorinha from 1992 to 1995, where his father was the kit man, and later spent two years with Nacional.

Achievements:

Over the course of his career, Portuguese footballer Cristiano Ronaldo has received five Ballon d'Or/FIFA Ballon d'Or awards,the most for a European player. Widely regarded as one of the greatest players of all time, Ronaldo holds the record for most goals and assists in the UEFA Champions League with 140 and 42 respectively, and the record for most goals in the UEFA European Championship with 14 goals, its qualification stage (40), and the FIFA Club World Cup with 7 goals, as well as most goals scored in a UEFA Champions League season with 17 goals, most international goals 127 goals, and most appearances in a European national team with 203 appearances. He has scored a record 864 senior career goals for club and country. Moreover, he is one of the few recorded players to have made over 1,100 professional career appearances.
